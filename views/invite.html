OUR STORY

How we met



We had actually met severally at LSO Bar and exchanged contacts and would occasionally just exchange pleasantries.

In March 2022, The normal pleasantries between myself and Albert had continued for a while until Victor (his friend) invited me for a barbecue they were organizing at their home and I gladly obliged ( after all, a night out was exactly what I need at the time albeit a little weird). I went to his house, which apparently was Albert’s and we had an amazing evening just talking, laughing and joking around. The company was great and there definitely was a connection. The following day, we started talking a lot more and spending a lot more time together and our friendship grew into the most beautiful love story…. The rest as they say is history.

On 17th of July 2023, it was Albert’s birthday and I had taken Albert out for dinner to celebrate and what turned out to be a surprise for Albert, ended up being a surprise for Me! Albert proposed in the most romantic way, on His Birthday, at the most private place and in the presence of family. That just simply defines who Albert is and his values and why he is my Home.

We can’t wait to have you share in our joy as we begin our journey of FOREVER !!
