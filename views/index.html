John and Vivian: 45th Wedding Anniversary

Most truly, the matrimonial story of John and Vivian is a delightful tidings worthy of acknowledgement and imitation. As all marriages have their ups and downs, so they had their fair share.

John had a very difficult start as early as age 12 when his mother passed away. But like Isaac in the Bible, he found solace 45 years ago in marrying Ama Ntiamoah Amankuo, affectionately called Vivian. Vivian also finally had a spiritual man to protect and nourish her spiritually. With Jehovah as the third cord, they started a humble marriage life.

With mutual love and sincerity, their union has lasted to witness its 45th anniversary. Today, we want to thank Jehovah for their faithful commitment to their vows, their worship of Jehovah, their children, careers, and many more. Their relationship is a beautiful demonstration of the strength of commitment, the joy of companionship, and the power of enduring love.

"I have learned how to think of others and to support them when in need," — Vivian heartily told John.

Johnny is known for his kindness, hospitality, reasonableness, and spirituality. His unwavering support for his wife and kids is truly inspiring. The simple yet profound moments they share are incredible.

"Me yere Ama," as John lovingly calls Vivian, shows how dear she is to him. She has been a meritorious wife in every aspect, offering genuine support in spiritual matters and nurturing discipline and training within the family.

The by-products of these good qualities are both fulfilling and inspiring. With Jehovah's backing, their six children have made remarkable contributions to Jehovah's organization. Their unwavering reliance on Jehovah has guided them in raising their family. All sons serve in various capacities in Jehovah's organization.

Join us in celebrating this wonderful milestone!

Let's honor their love, dedication, and spiritual journey together.
